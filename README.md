# Namgilmap

  Namgilmap is a prototype web app for recording and sharing digital financial asset information after death.

  It is not a legal will replacement. Instead, it helps users record where their digital financial assets and online accounts exist,
  how they want each asset to be handled, and what information can be shared with designated recipients after official death
  verification.

  ## Key Features

  - Record digital financial assets such as bank accounts, cards, e-wallets, crypto accounts, points, mileage, and subscriptions
  - Record digital accounts such as social media, YouTube, cloud storage, and blogs
  - Save subscription connection information, such as which card or bank account is linked to which service
  - Set posthumous handling preferences for each asset or account
  - Choose disclosure levels for each item
  - Designate recipients such as family members or legal representatives
  - Provide recipients with limited information after death verification

  ## Privacy Principle

  Namgilmap does not disclose or store sensitive credentials such as:

  - Passwords
  - OTP codes
  - Certificates
  - Private keys
  - Full card numbers
  - Detailed transaction history

  The service only provides allowed information such as:

  - Asset existence
  - Institution or platform name
  - Linked subscription service
  - Required documents
  - Basic handling procedures

  ## Purpose

  Many digital assets can be forgotten or lost because family members may not know they exist. Namgilmap helps users prepare a clear
  record in advance so that their family can find and handle digital financial assets safely and respectfully.

  ## Disclaimer

  This project is a prototype for educational and presentation purposes. It does not provide legal, financial, or inheritance execution
  services.
